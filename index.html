About Me

Dr. Muhammad Umair Raza completed his Ph.D in Computer Science and Technology at Shenzhen University, China in 2024. His research focuses on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), Interpretable Machine Learning, and Biomedical Image Processing for healthcare applications.

He has published 12 papers in top conferences including AAAI, IEEE BIBM, and IEEE ICDL, with over 100 citations. His work aims to make AI decisions in healthcare interpretable and trustworthy.
